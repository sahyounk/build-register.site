The Problem
$400B

Lost to construction disputes, defects, and unverifiable warranty claims every year. The industry has no standard, tamper-proof way to issue, track, or verify warranties.

Construction runs on paper.
Paper doesn't hold up.

  • ✗Warranties issued on PDFs that can be altered, lost, or disputed with no audit trail
  • ✗No single source of truth across developers, contractors, and property owners
  • ✗Claims processes are slow, opaque, and impossible to independently verify
  • ✗Regulators, insurers, and buyers have no independent verification layer
  • ✗Post-handover accountability collapses — nobody can prove who issued what and when
The Solution

On-chain registry. Off-chain simplicity.

Build Register creates an immutable, tamper-evident layer for construction warranty management — backed by smart contracts, accessible through a dashboard any stakeholder can use without knowing what a blockchain is.

📋

Project & Asset Registry

Every project, vendor, and asset registered on-chain with completion dates, responsible parties, and handover references.

🛡️

Warranty Certificates

Structural, waterproofing, HVAC, electrical — each warranty issued as an on-chain record with a unique certificate ID and verifiable expiry.

📂

Document Anchoring

Supporting documents hashed and anchored via IPFS — creating a tamper-evident paper trail tied to every warranty record.

⚡

Claims Management

File, track, and escalate warranty claims through a structured lifecycle — Logged → Submitted → Inspecting → Resolved.

✅

Instant Verification

Anyone can verify a warranty certificate using its ID. No login required. Built for owners, regulators, and insurers.

🔐

Role-Based Access

AccessManager contract governs who can issue warranties, log claims, and attach documents — no rogue edits, no data drift.
