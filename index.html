The problem

Construction records break when teams, documents and responsibilities fragment.

Warranties get buried in email. Certificates sit in PDFs no one can find. Handover packs are incomplete. Claims become painful because evidence is missing, late or disputed.

The answer

Build-Register turns every project into a durable digital record.

Create a structured source of truth for every asset and every project. Store the right files, anchor the critical ones, prove chain of custody and improve confidence for owners, operators, insurers and future buyers.

Platform capabilities

Built for the people who need proof, not just storage.

01

Project vaults

Dedicated repositories for each project, phase, unit or asset with role-based structure.

02

Warranty register

Track issuer, coverage, validity dates, exclusions and linked evidence in one view.

03

Audit trail

Record uploads, approvals, signoffs and amendments so responsibility stays visible.

04

Claims readiness

Surface the right files faster when defects, disputes or maintenance claims appear.
